HISTORY OF

RUGBY SEVENS

History

The history of rugby at the Olympic Games stretches over several periods. A men’s rugby union competition was first included at the 1900 Paris Games, and was contested again at the 1908, 1920 and 1924 Olympics. However, the sport was dropped from the Olympic programme following the 1924 Games in Paris. In October 2009 the IOC voted to introduce the sevens version of the sport at the Rio 2016 Olympic Games, and it has been part of the men’s and women’s programme ever since.
