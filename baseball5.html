HISTORY OF

BASEBALL 5

History

Baseball5, a simplified, five-player version of baseball, made its Olympic debut at the 2018 Youth Olympic Games in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Created to promote baseball in countries where the traditional version is less popular, Baseball5 offers a more accessible format with fewer equipment and space requirements. Its introduction aimed to engage younger audiences and broaden the sport's global appeal. Although Baseball5 has not yet been included as an official sport in the Summer Olympics, its presence in youth and regional events highlights its growing recognition and potential for future inclusion.
