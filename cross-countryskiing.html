    CROSS COUNTRY SKIING

    History

    Cross-country skiing is the oldest type of skiing. It emerged from a need to travel over snow-covered terrain and developed as a sport at the end of the 19th century.The men’s event debuted at the first Winter Olympic Games in Chamonix in 1924, and the women’s event debuted at the 1952 Oslo Games. The sport has traditionally been dominated by the Nordic countries.
